Before You Arrive...

Before you arrive please be sure to review our office policies and procedures, registration forms , and our list of services.  

Office Policy and Procedures

Pre-Visit Labs

All patients are required to get their labs in advance for physical and routine follow-up. Please let our associate know the reason for your visit and the nature of your chronic problems when you make your appointments so we can better serve you. Labs should be drawn 3-5 days prior to visit. If your labs are not available at the time of your visit, you may be asked to reschedule or make a lab only follow-up visit.

Lab Results

Genesis HCA only mails results on pap smears and mammograms. All other labs should be reviewed with providers in the context of a visit. This allows for more accurate communication. Patients can ask all of their questions and an appropriate follow-up plan can be made. Please make sure you make your follow-up appointment at your visit or immediately after you special testing is done.

Medication Refills

Genesis HCA does not do phone or fax refills. We will give you enough refills at your visit to get you to your next visit. This allows for accuracy in dosing, often lost or confused over the phone. It also makes sure that you get proper lab and side effect monitoring. Please make your medication refill appointments when you fill your last prescription at the pharmacy.

Physicals and Well Child Checks

One of the best ways to stay healthy is through preventive care. That's why we have special "Well-Care Visits" (Physicals & Well Child Checks) that are generally covered as part of a Preventive Services benefit.

There is no copay, coinsurance, or deductible for the benefit. Well-care visits focus on what you can do to prevent future disease or injury, or catch health problems before they become serious.

You may have health issues or conditions you're managing, but the focus of a well-care visit is to avoid future health issues.
